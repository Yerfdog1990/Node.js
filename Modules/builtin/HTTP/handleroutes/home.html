Springfield Academy

Excellence in Education Since 1990

Our History

Founded in 1990, Springfield Academy has been a cornerstone of educational excellence for over three decades. Starting with just 50 students, we have grown into a prestigious institution serving over 1,000 students while maintaining our commitment to quality education.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens through innovative teaching methods and a supportive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that nurtures creative thinking, promotes academic excellence, and develops well-rounded individuals prepared for future challenges.

Our Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • Core Academic Subjects (Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Social Studies)
  • Advanced Placement (AP) Programs
  • STEAM Education Integration
  • Arts and Music Programs
  • Physical Education and Sports
  • Extra-curricular Activities
